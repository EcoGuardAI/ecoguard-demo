### ✨ AST Research Integration

- **Enable**: `--enable-ast-research`
- **Depth**: `--ast-depth basic|detailed|comprehensive`
- **Patterns**: `--enable-pattern-analysis`
- **Metrics**: `--enable-complexity-metrics`
